About  Us

World Class Qulity & Beautiful Designs

“CLEAR VISION OPTICS, established in 2005 is a world of Sunglasses and vision accessoriess, presenting you everything latest in Sunglasses, Frames and lenses, helping you look smart and stand apart in the crowd”

Established in 2005, CLEAR VISION OPTICS, is a one-stop trusted shop for branded eyeglasses, sunglasses, prescription lenses and frames. We offer all brands, latest designs at affordable prices. Our goal is to make it easy to understand your requirement, easy to place an order and offer a on-time delivery. We are dealers of well-known brands such as Ray-Ban, Police, Bvlgari, Dior, Prada, Silhouette, Swarovski, TagHeuer, Carrera, Burberry, Jaguar, Calvin Klein, Nike, Adidas, Armani Exchange, Guess, Stepper, DKNY, Oakley, Roberto Cavalli, Versace, Emporio Armani, D & G, Vogue, Lacoste and many more.
We offer an entire range of contact lenses which includes visual correction, cosmetic, toric and bifocal variants. With the lenses, we also offer different types of contact lens care solutions from Bausch & Lomb, Ciba vision
